Taiga.
Date - 02.04.21
Written by Нгуен Чан Хоанг Чунг
Tags - #Forest, #Taiga, #Greenery

Taiga is a biome characterized by a predominance of coniferous forests
formed mainly by boreal species of spruce, fir, larch and pine
The word "taiga" also denotes one of the geographical subzones
northern temperate zone
Tundra.
Date - 02.04.21
Written by Нгуен Чан Хоанг Чунг
Tags - # Forest, # Tundra, # North

Tundra is a kind of natural zones lying outside the northern limits of forest vegetation
spaces with permafrost soil, not flooded by sea or river waters
The tundra is located north of the taiga zone
By the nature of the surface of the tundra there are swampy, peaty, rocky
